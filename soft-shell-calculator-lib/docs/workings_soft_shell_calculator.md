- the soft shell calculator uses ResiPowerDrill measurements of wooden foundation poles.
- this is done to determine the quality of the wood.
- this method of research prevents having to take samples of the wood, which is more destructive.
- its mainly done by measuring the resistance of the wood.
- makes an estimate of the soft shell (deteriorated profile)
